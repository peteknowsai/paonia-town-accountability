# STR Initiative

**Status:** Rejected by Town Clerk (May 25, 2025)
**The first initiative in the fight — the one that exposed the pattern.**

## What It Proposed
- Protect resident right to host guests in principal residence
- Eliminate all fees for resident-occupied short-term rentals
- Phase out non-resident STRs by June 2026
- Grandfather multi-family/commercial indefinitely

## The Rejection
[[Samira Vetter]] rejected the initiative claiming it would "establish fees" — when the text explicitly **eliminated** fees. This was either incompetence or bad faith.

## Context
- April 2025: **70.4% of voters (381-160)** rejected the Board's restrictive STR ordinance
- The Board's approach was overwhelmingly repudiated at the ballot box
- The initiative offered a citizen alternative
- Rejection blocked the democratic alternative after voters already rejected the Board's approach
