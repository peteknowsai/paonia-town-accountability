# Email Transparency Initiative

**Status:** Submitted September 3, 2025 (omnibus)
❓ *Current status unknown*

## What It Proposes
- 90-day minimum retention of all electronic communications
- Quarterly public reports of email metrics
- Automatic CORA compliance
- Criminal penalties for deliberate deletion

## Why It's Needed
The Town claims Microsoft 365 audit logs "don't exist" — a technical impossibility. M365 automatically generates email logs, login records, document history, meeting records, and security logs. They cannot be fully disabled.
