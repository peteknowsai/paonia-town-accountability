# Trustee Protection Initiative

**Status:** Submitted September 3, 2025 (omnibus)
❓ *Current status unknown*

## What It Proposes
- Require **unanimous consent** for removal of elected trustees (not just 4 votes)
- Protect CORA requests, oversight, questioning staff as protected activities
- Prevent administrator threats/coercion against elected officials
- Provide due process protections

## Why It's Needed
[[Bill Brunner]] was elected with 67.7% of the vote and removed 3 months later by a 4-1 board vote after [[Stefen Wynn]] conditioned his employment on Brunner's removal. Brunner's offense was exercising oversight.
