# Colorado Drought 2026

## Current Conditions
- Lake Powell inflows: **43% of normal** (April-July 2026 forecast)
- Over **80% of SNOTEL stations** in snow drought (Jan 2026)
- Colorado water officials describe forecasts as **"grim"** (Colorado Sun, Jan 28, 2026)
- Snow drought defined as SWE below 20th percentile
- More rain than snow in Water Year 2026 so far (Oct 2025-Jan 2026)

## Impact on Paonia
- Water from mountain springs — directly vulnerable to reduced snowpack
- Town already imposed water restrictions (Aug 2025) for construction, not drought
- Trees dying across town with no maintenance program
- Water system infrastructure from early 1900s — cannot handle stress
