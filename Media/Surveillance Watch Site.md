# Surveillance Watch Site

**Project:** Paonia Surveillance Watch
**Repo:** `~/Projects/paonia-surveillance-watch/`
**Stack:** Next.js 15, Convex, Tailwind CSS 4, Cloudflare Workers

## Concept
Visual podcast feed of ~12 mini-episodes (2 min each) with AI art and AI voice, plus a digital petition. Scroll, tap play, get informed. No reading required.
