# Federal Civil Rights Exposure

If the Town continues to obstruct citizen initiatives, federal civil rights law applies.

## 42 U.S.C. § 1983 — Civil Rights Action
- Deprivation of constitutional rights under color of law
- The right to petition the government is a First Amendment right
- Systematic obstruction of the initiative process = deprivation of petition rights
- Damages, injunctive relief, attorney fees available
- ❓ *Any precedent for § 1983 claims based on initiative obstruction specifically?*

## 18 U.S.C. § 241 — Conspiracy Against Rights
- Criminal statute
- "Two or more persons conspire to injure, oppress, threaten, or intimidate any person...in the free exercise or enjoyment of any right or privilege secured to him by the Constitution"
- Pattern of coordinated obstruction (Buchner + Vetter + Board) could qualify
- ❓ *FBI referral threshold — what evidence standard?*

## 18 U.S.C. § 242 — Deprivation of Rights Under Color of Law
- Criminal statute
- "Whoever, under color of any law...willfully subjects any person...to the deprivation of any rights"
- Town officials acting in official capacity rejecting initiatives on false grounds
- ❓ *Has this ever been used for initiative obstruction?*
