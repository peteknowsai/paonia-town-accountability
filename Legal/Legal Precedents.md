# Legal Precedents

Key Colorado cases for initiative rights and municipal law.

## Initiative Power

### *Vagneur v. City of Aspen*, 295 P.3d 493 (Colo. 2013)
- Initiative power must be "liberally construed" in favor of the people
- **Caution:** This is a home rule case. Buchner has been misapplying it to Paonia (statutory town). The liberal construction principle applies, but the home rule limitations do not.

### *City of Montrose v. Pub. Utils. Comm'n*, 629 P.2d 619 (Colo. 1981)
- Establishes the **Blackwell test** for legislative vs. administrative:
  1. Creates permanent law
  2. Declares policy
  3. Applies generally
- Both initiatives satisfy all three prongs

### *Carpenter v. United States*, 585 U.S. 296 (2018)
- Cloud-based surveillance constitutes a search under the Fourth Amendment
- Relevant to camera ban arguments about persistent monitoring
