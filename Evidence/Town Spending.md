# Town Spending

The contrast between what Paonia spends on equipment/surveillance vs. community needs.

## Surveillance & Equipment Spending
| Item | Cost | Public Process |
|------|------|----------------|
| Verkada cameras | ~$50,000 + annual cloud licenses | Minimal/none |
| Daxbot ADA assessment (MHAC) | $33,500 | Board tentatively accepted |
| Street sweeper lease-purchase | $200-350K (est.) | March 2026, unbudgeted mid-year |
| Patrol car replacement (annual) | $50,000/yr | 5-year program |
| Patrol car outfitting (annual) | $15,000/yr | 5-year program |
| Cellular water meters | $50,000 + recurring monthly | Ongoing |
| SaaS/technology subscriptions | $177,000/yr | Grows annually |
| **Total recurring new costs** | **~$950K-1M/yr** | |

## What's Underfunded
| Need | Budget | Reality |
|------|--------|---------|
| Street resurfacing | $35,000/yr | Fraction of actual need |
| Tree maintenance | $0 dedicated | Trees dying in historic drought |
| Water system (beyond Phase 1) | $0 for Phases 2-5 | 20-year CIP, tens of millions needed |
| Wastewater assessment | $12,000 (CCTV only) | Full PNA not complete |
| Bridge repair | Waiting on grants | $1.96M needed, 75% unfunded |

## 10 Low-Cost Alternatives (from fiscal analysis)
All 10 combined: ~$25-45K total
1. Community tree watering & mulching — $3-5K
2. Bioswales & rain gardens — $500-3K each
3. Volunteer leak reporting — $500
4. Local-labor ditch maintenance — $5-8K/yr
5. Community tool library — $2K startup
6. Natural erosion control (willow stakes) — $1-3K
7. Greywater education program — $1.5K
8. Youth conservation corps — $8-10K/season
9. Permeable parking & recycled millings — $3-7K
10. Neighbor water sharing network — free
