# Samira Vetter

**Role:** Town Clerk, Paonia

## Key Incident
**May 25, 2025:** Rejected the citizen STR initiative claiming it would "establish fees" — when the initiative text explicitly *eliminated* fees. The misreading was so fundamental it raises questions about whether the rejection was made in good faith.

This came just 2 months after **70.4% of voters (381-160)** rejected the Board's restrictive STR ordinance.
