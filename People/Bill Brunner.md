# Bill Brunner

**Role:** Former Trustee, Paonia (removed August 15, 2024)

## Background
- November 2018: Won a CORA lawsuit against Paonia. Judge Schultz found town officials "failed to exercise reasonable diligence or reasonable inquiry." Town paid his legal fees.
- April 2024: Ran for trustee on transparency platform
- Elected with **67.7% of the vote** (227 citizens)

## The Removal
**August 15, 2024** — Board voted 4-1 to remove Brunner just 3 months after election.

**For removal:** Stelter, Czech, Valentine, Hunter
**Against:** Swartz
**Could not vote:** Mayor Smith (charging officer), Brunner (his own removal)

### The Trigger
Town Administrator [[Stefen Wynn]] threatened to resign, conditioning his continued employment on Brunner's removal. The Board chose the unelected administrator over the elected trustee.

### The Charges (vague)
- "Harassment" of staff
- "Inappropriate criticism"
- "Disruptive behavior"
- No specific examples provided publicly

### What Brunner Actually Did
- Asked questions
- Requested documents
- Exercised oversight
- Exactly what 227 voters elected him to do

## Impact
Sent a clear message to all remaining trustees: ask uncomfortable questions and you'll be removed. Chilled all oversight.
