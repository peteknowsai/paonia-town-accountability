# Stefen Wynn

**Role:** Town Administrator AND Town Treasurer, Paonia (since July 12, 2023)
**Appointed by:** Resolution 08-2023 (July 11, 2023)

## Compensation (see [[Wynn Compensation]] for full details)
- FY-2026 total across all funds: **~$139,600** (direct salary line items only, benefits separate)

- No documented Board authorization for the shift
- Employment contract negotiated in executive session (June 27, 2023), not publicly available

## Key Actions
- Threatened resignation to force [[Bill Brunner]]'s removal — Board complied (Aug 2024)
- Acknowledged Town was "caught off guard" by early Daxbot robot arrival (GJ Sentinel, July 12, 2025)
- Approved $50,000 Verkada camera purchase
- Proposed unbudgeted street sweeper lease-purchase (March 2026)
- Grew admin from 4.0 to 6.3 FTE (57.5% increase in 3 years)
- Prepares the budget that determines his own salary allocation across funds

## Background
- Previously **fired from Neptune Beach, FL** for "flagrant neglect of duty" and "willful misconduct"
- Holds MPA from Indiana University South Bend
- Claims "email not working" to explain billing failures
- Appointed to both TA and Treasurer roles simultaneously (concentration of financial authority)

- Total appropriations grew from ~$9.2M to $16.16M under his leadership
- Grant dependency: 69% of revenue from external sources
- Debt service: $330K (FY-2024) → $822K (FY-2026), +149%
- Personnel cost: +32% in 3 years while population grew 1.1%
- Water system Phase 1 mostly not started despite years of planning
