# Stefen Wynn — Neptune Beach Background

## Timeline
- **Jan 6, 2020:** Sworn in as Neptune Beach, FL City Manager
- **During tenure:** Approved permit for 181,000-gallon water tank behind Publix — neighbors called it "big green monster" (33ft diameter, 29ft tall). Also neglected Senior Center project (unfinished 2+ years later).
- **Jan 17, 2023:** City Council voted **unanimously** to fire him
  - Charges: "flagrant neglect of duty" and "willful misconduct which substantially impairs or damages the interests or reputation of the city"
  - City attorney recommended termination for cause
- **June 2023:** Filed whistleblower retaliation lawsuit claiming he was fired for reporting fraud
  - City's defense: "we would have fired you anyway, regardless"
- **July 12, 2023:** Started as Paonia Town Administrator — 6 months after firing
