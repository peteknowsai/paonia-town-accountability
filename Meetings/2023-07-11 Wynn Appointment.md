# Meeting: July 11, 2023

**Type:** Regular Town Board Meeting
**Key event:** Resolution 08-2023 — Wynn formally appointed

## What Happened
- Resolution 08-2023 passed appointing Stefen Wynn as **Town Administrator AND Town Treasurer**
- Effective date: July 12, 2023
- Public reception held July 12
- Also designated Wynn as Town's ADA coordinator

## Resolution 08-2023 Details
- References Resolution 07-2023 (appointed Leslie Klusmire as interim)
- States interim contract term complete, creating vacancies
- Appoints Wynn to both offices
- **NO salary, compensation, benefits, or fund allocation specified**
- **NO employment terms beyond title and start date**
- Signed by Mayor, attested by Town Clerk

## What's Missing
- Compensation terms (in contract only, not public)
- Duration of appointment
- Fund allocation methodology
- Performance review process
- Termination provisions
